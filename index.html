ABOUT

Healthy Hub helps you to improve your sports experience. Here, you can get informed about your body and your health, determine the most suitable target for you, learn your calorie needs according to your goal, make an exercise plan and see exercise forms accordingly. You can also learn the answers of the most popular questions about fitness and contact us to get more information.


  • Calculate your BMI to determine your goal.
  • Calculate your caloric needs and macros due to your goal.
  • Create a workout program.
  • Learn exercise forms from the exercise guide.

BODY MASS INDEX (BMI)


Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women. Calculate your BMI to determine your goal.

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI < 18.5 (Gain weight)
  • Normal weight: 18.5 < BMI < 24.9 (Maintain)
  • Overweight: BMI > 24.9 (Loss weight)
